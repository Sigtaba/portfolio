About Me

I was born and raised in St.Louis, Missouri. After highschool, I moved to Oxford, Ohio to attend college at Miami University for business, but after a semester I realized Miami was not a culture fit for me, and I needed to be somewhere more diverse. I transferred to DePaul University and joined the economic honors program and graduated with a BSC in Honors Economics. After college I moved back to St. Louis and worked at various ecommerce positions, ranging from family businesses to more well known companies. After a couple years of living in St. Louis I moved out to Portland, Oregon to work as an eCommerce Coordinator at CRKT. There, I coordinated launch and maintenance development for the company's website. While working with their outside dev teams, I realized I was much more interested in creating websites rather than managing them. After about a year of working there I applied to Epicodus where I am now studying CSS and Web Design.
